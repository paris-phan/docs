The entire development environment is codified in [`dotfiles`](https://github.com/paris-phan/dotfiles) and reproducible from a clean M4 Mac with a one-command bootstrap (`install.sh`, 15 ordered steps).

## The machine

- **Hardware:** Apple Silicon M4 Mac Mini (Darwin 25.5.0)
- **Shell:** `fish` (login) with `fisher` plugins; `zsh` fallback
- **Prompt:** [starship](https://starship.rs/)

## Bootstrap a clean machine

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clone dotfiles">
    Pull the `dotfiles` repo onto the fresh machine.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the installer">
    `install.sh` runs 15 ordered steps — Homebrew packages and casks, symlinks, macOS defaults, and Conda restore.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to services">
    Authenticate `gh` and Claude; the rest of the configuration is already in place.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Because the machine is rebuildable from config, experiments are cheap — a broken setup is one bootstrap away from clean.
</Tip>
